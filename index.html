Our Motto

To try connect people who want to help those who themself were helping hands of someone else. By our platform we we want to connect Old aged people who need help with those who are willing to help our elderly people.

How do we work?

When you want to donate or provide something, we ask you to share the details of the thing you want to donate. Once your complete adding details we save it in our system, on the other hand when a person in needs some kind of support and they find the thing the want as support, we will show them your name with all the other people who are interested in donating the same thing.
